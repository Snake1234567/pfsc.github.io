SUPPORT GROUP

At ..., a formal support group will be led by one of the volunteers at the Wellness Center. The patients will determine what they want to discuss, and everyone will go at their own pace. The volunteer may mediate by providing topic questions or small group discussions to facilitate discussions if patients have nothing to say. This will be a time to talk about shared experiences, get to know other patients, and create support systems within the group.

A PATIENT DRIVEN EXPERIENCE

The patient determines what they make out of the resources the Stroke Wellness Center provides. All exercises and social interaction will be overseen by both undergraduate and graduate volunteers who have been trained to assist if something goes wrong, but patients determine what exercises they do, who they talk to, and what they want to get out of their experience
