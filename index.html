The Pittsburgh Free Stroke Clinic is a free clinic for stroke survivors who do not have access to proper healthcare. Simple motor exercises are given to patients by trained undergraduate volunteers to help increase the quality of life for survivors.

*All patients are required to fill out a consent and liability form upon arrival to the clinic. Forms will be provided
